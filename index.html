Alcestide is an indie music project of unspecified genre, prone to electronic/ambient music most of the times.

You can check out my music at SoundCloud.

Decay, rottenness, woe and despair, you name it.

A compendium of mental instability, wrath, vulnerability surging from an incomplete and distorted sense of narcissistic perfection.

Who is Alcestide? It's nothing at all, yet it's everything at the same time.

Everything there's to wish for but too unstable to grasp.

A loud, pounding heart, placed under the floorboards of your bedroom.

The mongoose you want under the house when the snakes slither by.
